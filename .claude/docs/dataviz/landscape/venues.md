# Visualization Venues, Competitions & Community

Where the field publishes, competes, and gathers. Load for context on state-of-the-art and who's winning.

## Academic Venues (Ranked by Impact on Practice)

| Venue | Type | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| **IEEE VIS** | Conference (annual) | Full spectrum viz research. Unified InfoVis/VAST/SciVis since 2021. 2025: Vienna. |
| **IEEE TVCG** | Journal (continuous) | Premier viz journal. VIS papers appear here. |
| **ACM CHI** | Conference (annual) | HCI + viz intersection. Accessibility, interaction, user studies. |
| **EuroVis** | Conference (annual) | European viz community. Published in Computer Graphics Forum. |
| **ACM UIST** | Conference (annual) | Novel interaction techniques. |
| **ACM SIGMOD** | Conference (annual) | Database + viz intersection. Mosaic demo 2025. |
| **ICLR** | Conference (annual) | ML + viz (ChartMimic benchmark 2025). |

## Hot Research Areas (2024-2026)

1. **AI + Visualization** (hottest) — DracoGPT, LIDA, Data-Formulator, ChartMimic, LLM chain design. Agent-generated dashboards becoming a well-studied problem.
2. **Uncertainty Visualization** — Padilla (frequency framing), progressive viz survey. Psychology-grounded approaches.
3. **Accessibility** — Tactile charts (Utah), rich screen reader experiences (UW), sonification, audio data narratives.
4. **Scalable Interactive Viz** — Mosaic + DuckDB, Mosaic Selections, Falcon. Browser-as-thin-client over local databases.
5. **Immersive Analytics** — AR/VR data visualization. 3-5 years from consumer health use.

## Competitions & Awards

### Active & Prestigious

| Competition | Cycle | What It Rewards |
|-------------|-------|----------------|
| **Information is Beautiful Awards** (Kantar) | Annual (~1000 submissions) | Beauty, storytelling, impact, innovation. Categories include Science/Health. 2024 Gold: "Zoonotic Web" by Liuhuaying Yang. |
| **IEEE VIS Best Paper** | Annual | Peer-reviewed research excellence. |
| **Iron Viz** (Tableau) | Annual | Live 20-min dashboard creation. Design, analysis, storytelling. 2025 champion: Bo McCready. |
| **Outlier Conference** (DVS) | Annual | Talks on iteration, feedback, constraints. 2026 theme: "The Final Draft." |
| **ACM CHI Best Paper** | Annual | HCI research including viz. |

### Paused or Evolved

| Competition | Status | Notes |
|-------------|--------|-------|
| **Malofiej Awards** | Paused since Oct 2021 | Was "the Pulitzers for infographics." No resumption confirmed. |
| **OpenVis Conf** | Evolved | Original (Bocoup) inactive. Open Visualization Collaborator Summit (OpenJS Foundation) is successor. Oct 2025, Seattle/Google. Toolmaker-focused. |
